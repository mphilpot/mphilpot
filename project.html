Selling Decal Plugin
A plugin that will allow me to sell all my loot with out having to actually pay attention in Asherons Call. This will allow the user to set a sell profile, that will sell your "loot" until your packs are full of pyreal. Personally i want this after my nice long loot runs so it takes me about 1/3 of the time to actually sell and get back to having some fun ;). After the first version of this i'll start working towards automated selling, ie. the plugin will cruse around the town selling your "loot" for you while you chat away =).
